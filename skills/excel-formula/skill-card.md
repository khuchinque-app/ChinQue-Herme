## Description: <br>
Generate Excel formulas from descriptions and diagnose spreadsheet errors, including VLOOKUP help, error debugging, formula conversion, bilingual documentation, and AI-assisted spreadsheet guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[xueyetianya](https://clawhub.ai/user/xueyetianya) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Employees, analysts, and spreadsheet users use this skill to generate, explain, debug, and convert Excel, Google Sheets, and WPS formulas from natural-language requests. It also provides quick-reference guidance for common lookup, conditional, text, date, template, and PivotTable workflows. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The command-line helper may keep a local history of formula snippets or search terms on disk. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering sensitive workbook data, set EXCEL_DIR to a controlled location, or clear the history file after use. <br>
